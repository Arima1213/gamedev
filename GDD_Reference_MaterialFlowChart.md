## 1. Material Types

### 1.1 Material Categories

**Raw Materials (Tier 1):**
- Gathered directly from world (mining, monster drops, fishing)
- Cannot be used directly for crafting (most items)
- Must be processed first
- Examples: Iron Ore, Copper Ore, Wood Logs, Raw Cotton

**Processed Materials (Tier 2):**
- Created from raw materials via NPCs or crafting stations
- Used in actual equipment/item crafting
- Examples: Iron Ingot, Copper Bar, Planks, Cloth

**Refined Materials (Tier 3):**
- Advanced processing, multiple steps
- Used for high-tier equipment
- Examples: Steel Ingot, Mithril Alloy, Reinforced Leather

**Special Materials (Unique):**
- Cannot be crafted, only obtained from specific sources
- Boss drops, event rewards, rare fishing
- Examples: Dragon Heart Gem, Phoenix Feather, Void Crystal

## 2. Raw Materials (Gathering)

### 2.1 Mining Materials

#### Ores (Need Smelting)

**Iron Ore**
- **Source:** Mining stone/granite rocks in dungeons (Mining Zone A-B)
- **Drop Rate:** 30-40% from appropriate rocks
- **Monster Drop:** Goblin Warrior (40%), Orc Warrior (70%)
- **Processing:** Smelt 5 Iron Ore → 1 Iron Ingot (at Blacksmith NPC)
- **Used For:** Cannot use directly, must smelt first

**Copper Ore**
- **Source:** Mining stone rocks (Mining Zone A)
- **Drop Rate:** 50% from stone nodes
- **Monster Drop:** Common low-level enemies
- **Processing:** Smelt 5 Copper Ore → 1 Copper Ingot
- **Used For:** Cannot use directly

**Gold Ore**
- **Source:** Rare granite rocks (Mining Zone C)
- **Drop Rate:** 10% from granite
- **Processing:** Smelt 5 Gold Ore → 1 Gold Ingot
- **Used For:** Jewelry crafting, premium equipment

**Titanium Ore**
- **Source:** Obsidian rocks (Mining Zone D)
- **Drop Rate:** 8% from obsidian
- **Monster Drop:** Lava Golem (60%)
- **Processing:** Smelt 8 Titanium Ore → 1 Titanium Ingot
- **Used For:** High-tier weapons/armor

**Mithril Ore**
- **Source:** Mythic rocks (Mining Zone E), boss loot
- **Drop Rate:** 3% from mythic nodes
- **Processing:** Smelt 10 Mithril Ore + 1 Magic Catalyst → 1 Mithril Ingot
- **Used For:** Legendary equipment

---

#### Gems (Ready to Use)

**Ruby, Emerald, Sapphire, Diamond, etc.**
- **Source:** Mining gem-rich rocks, geodes
- **Processing:** Can be used raw OR cut by Jeweler NPC for better effects
- **Cut Gems:** 1 Raw Gem + 100 IRF → 1 Cut Gem (+50% effectiveness)

---

### 2.2 Monster Drop Materials

#### Leather
**Source:** Animal-type monsters
- Wolf (50% drop, 1-3 units)
- Bear (80% drop, 2-4 units)
- Goblin Scout (25% drop, 1-2 units)

**Processing:** 
- Raw Leather → Used directly for basic armor
- 5 Leather + 2 Oil → 1 Reinforced Leather (for better armor)

**Dragon Leather**
**Source:** Dragon bosses only
**Processing:** Cannot be processed, used as-is for legendary armor

---

#### String
**Source:** Spider and insect monsters
- Cave Spider (80% drop, 4-8 units) - **PRIMARY SOURCE**
- Giant Spider (90% drop, 6-10 units)

**Used For:** Bows, fishing rods, cloth crafting

---

#### Cloth
**Source:** Humanoid monsters
- Goblin Scout (60% drop, 1-2 units)
- Goblin Warrior (70% drop, 2-3 units)
- Orc (80% drop, 3-5 units)

**Processing:**
- Goblin Cloth → Used directly for basic armor
- 10 Cloth + 5 String → 1 Silk Cloth (premium light armor)

---

### 2.3 Farming Materials

**Crops**
- **Source:** Farming on Private Island
- **Processing:** Harvested crops can be used directly OR cooked into food buffs
- **Examples:** Wheat → Bread, Tomato → Food buff

---

### 2.4 Fishing Materials

**Fish**
- **Source:** Fishing in Ocean/River zones
- **Processing:** Used as familiar food OR cooked into food buffs
- **Special:** Some fish give crafting materials when processed

## 3. Processed Materials (Smelting/Processing)

### 3.1 Metal Ingots

#### Iron Ingot
**Recipe:**
- 5 Iron Ore → 1 Iron Ingot
- **Station:** Blacksmith NPC (Smelting service)
- **Cost:** 50 IRF per ingot
- **Time:** 10 seconds

**Used For:**
- Iron Sword (20 Iron Ingot)
- Iron Chestplate (15 Iron Ingot)
- Iron Pickaxe (20 Iron Ingot)
- Basic Fishing Rod (5 Iron Ingot)

---

#### Copper Ingot
**Recipe:**
- 5 Copper Ore → 1 Copper Ingot
- **Station:** Blacksmith NPC
- **Cost:** 30 IRF per ingot
- **Time:** 8 seconds

**Used For:**
- Copper Sword (15 Copper Ingot)
- Copper Pickaxe (10 Copper Ingot)
- Wiring (decoration crafting)

---

#### Steel Ingot
**Recipe:**
- 3 Iron Ingot + 1 Coal → 1 Steel Ingot
- **Station:** Blacksmith NPC (Advanced Smelting)
- **Cost:** 100 IRF per ingot
- **Time:** 30 seconds

**Used For:**
- Steel Sword (25 Steel Ingot)
- Steel Armor (40-60 Steel Ingot)
- Advanced tools

---

#### Gold Ingot
**Recipe:**
- 5 Gold Ore → 1 Gold Ingot
- **Station:** Blacksmith NPC
- **Cost:** 200 IRF per ingot
- **Time:** 20 seconds

**Used For:**
- Gold Pickaxe (30 Gold Ingot)
- Jewelry crafting
- Premium decorations

---

#### Titanium Ingot
**Recipe:**
- 8 Titanium Ore + 2 Coal → 1 Titanium Ingot
- **Station:** Master Blacksmith NPC (unlocks Level 30)
- **Cost:** 500 IRF per ingot
- **Time:** 60 seconds

**Used For:**
- Titanium weapons (epic tier)
- Titanium armor (epic tier)
- High-end tools

---

#### Mithril Ingot
**Recipe:**
- 10 Mithril Ore + 1 Magic Catalyst + 5 Coal → 1 Mithril Ingot
- **Station:** Legendary Blacksmith (unlocks Level 40)
- **Cost:** 2,000 IRF per ingot
- **Time:** 120 seconds

**Used For:**
- Mithril Blade (legendary weapon)
- Mithril armor (legendary tier)
- Mythic tools

---

#### Adamantite Ingot
**Recipe:**
- 15 Adamantite Ore + 3 Magic Catalyst + 10 Coal → 1 Adamantite Ingot
- **Station:** Legendary Blacksmith
- **Cost:** 5,000 IRF per ingot
- **Time:** 180 seconds

**Used For:**
- Dragon equipment
- Mythic tier weapons/armor
- Ultimate tools

---

### 3.2 Processed Organic Materials

#### Reinforced Leather
**Recipe:**
- 5 Leather + 2 Oil → 1 Reinforced Leather
- **Station:** Leatherworker NPC
- **Cost:** 150 IRF
- **Time:** 30 seconds

**Used For:** Medium/Heavy armor (rare+ tier)

---

#### Silk Cloth
**Recipe:**
- 10 Cloth + 5 String → 1 Silk Cloth
- **Station:** Tailor NPC
- **Cost:** 200 IRF
- **Time:** 45 seconds

**Used For:** Premium light armor, bags, decorations

---

#### Planks
**Recipe:**
- 4 Wood Logs → 10 Planks
- **Station:** Carpenter NPC OR Workbench
- **Cost:** 10 IRF
- **Time:** 5 seconds

**Used For:** Furniture, structures, bows, fishing rods

## 5. Crafting Requirements

### 5.1 Common Tier Equipment

**Wooden Sword**
- 10 Wood → 10 Planks (process)
- 2 Stone (raw)
- **Total:** Need wood chopping + stone mining

**Copper Sword**
- 15 Copper Ore → 3 Copper Ingot (process, 5:1 ratio)
- 5 Wood → 5 Planks
- 3 Leather (from wolf/bear)
- **Total:** Mining + monster hunting + wood chopping

---

### 5.2 Rare Tier Equipment

**Iron Sword**
- 100 Iron Ore → 20 Iron Ingot (process, 5:1 ratio)
- 10 Coal (for smelting, from mining)
- 5 Leather (from monsters)
- **Total:** Heavy dungeon mining + monster hunting

---

### 5.3 Epic Tier Equipment

**Steel Greatsword**
- 200 Iron Ore → 40 Iron Ingot → 13 Steel Ingot (multi-step: 5→1→3:1)
- 20 Coal
- 10 Reinforced Leather (50 Leather + 20 Oil → 10 Reinforced)
- 5 Titanium Ingot (40 Titanium Ore → 5 Ingot)
- **Total:** MASSIVE material investment

---

### 5.4 Legendary Tier Equipment

**Mithril Blade**
- 200 Mithril Ore → 20 Mithril Ingot
- 20 Magic Catalyst (boss/event rewards)
- 100 Coal
- 10 Cut Diamonds (10 Raw Diamond + processing)
- 5 Legendary Leather (dragon drop)
- **Total:** Endgame farming required

## 7. Material Balance Notes

### 7.1 Iron Ore vs Iron Ingot Confusion - CLARIFIED

**RULE:** 
- Monsters drop **Iron Ore** (raw material)
- Crafting recipes require **Iron Ingot** (processed material)
- Players MUST visit Blacksmith NPC to smelt ore into ingots

**Example Player Flow:**
1. Player kills Goblin Warrior → Gets 3 Iron Ore
2. Player mines dungeon rocks → Gets 10 Iron Ore (total: 13)
3. Player visits Blacksmith NPC → "Smelt 10 Iron Ore → 2 Iron Ingot" (5:1 ratio, costs 100 IRF)
4. Player now has 2 Iron Ingot + 3 leftover Iron Ore
5. Player crafts Iron Sword → Needs 20 Iron Ingot → Player needs to farm 90 more Iron Ore

**Why This System:**
- Creates NPC interaction (economy sink)
- Adds time/cost investment (balancing)
- Makes high-tier items feel earned
- Prevents instant crafting after mining

---

### 7.2 Common Bottlenecks (By Design)

**Coal Shortage:**
- Needed for Steel/Titanium smelting
- Only 25% drop rate from rocks
- Forces players to mine more (time sink)
- Can buy from shop (IRF sink)

**Leather Shortage:**
- Needed for almost all armor
- Forces players to hunt monsters (not just mine)
- Encourages dungeon runs
- Good familiar food (competing demand)

**String Shortage:**
- Only from spiders
- Spiders in mid-level caves
- Forces progression through content
- Needed for bows and fishing rods (high demand)

**Magic Catalyst Shortage:**
- Boss drops only (or expensive shop)
- Limits legendary crafting
- Creates endgame grind
- Encourages boss farming

---

### 7.3 Material Sinks (Why Players Need Lots)

**Equipment Crafting:**
- Multiple armor pieces per set (3-4 pieces)
- Weapons break (durability)
- Upgrade system eats materials (+1 to +10 enhancement)

**Private Island Building:**
- Furniture needs wood/stone/metal
- Structures need massive amounts
- Decorations create ongoing demand

**Familiar Care:**
- Daily feeding uses crops/fish/meat
- Multiple familiars = high demand

**Trading Economy:**
- Sell excess materials for IRF
- Buy rare materials from other players
