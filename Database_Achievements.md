## Achievement System Overview

### How Achievements Work

**Purpose:**
- Track player milestones and accomplishments
- Reward dedicated players with titles, cosmetics, and bonuses
- Provide long-term goals beyond leveling
- Showcase player prowess and dedication

**Achievement Rewards:**
- **Titles:** Display above player name (purely cosmetic)
- **Cosmetic Items:** Exclusive decorations, skins, emotes
- **Stat Bonuses:** Permanent small buffs
- **Achievement Points:** Currency for exclusive rewards
- **IRF:** In-game currency rewards
- **Exclusive Access:** Unlock special content

**Achievement Tiers:**
| Rarity | Points Awarded | Difficulty | Examples |
|--------|----------------|------------|----------|
| Common | 10 AP | Easy milestones | First harvest, first catch |
| Rare | 25 AP | Medium effort | 100 monsters killed, 50 fish caught |
| Epic | 50 AP | Challenging | 1,000 dungeons cleared, all crop types |
| Legendary | 100 AP | Very hard | Solo party dungeon, 10M IRF earned |
| Mythic | 250 AP | Extreme dedication | All achievements completed, 100 in all proficiencies |

## Developer Notes

### Adding New Achievements

**Steps:**
1. Define achievement ID (ACH_CATEGORY_###)
2. Set name, description, requirements
3. Assign rarity tier (points)
4. Create rewards (title, items, buffs)
5. Implement tracking system
6. Design icon/badge
7. Add to achievement database
8. Test unlock trigger

**Balance Considerations:**
- Common: Achievable in first play session
- Rare: 1-2 weeks of casual play
- Epic: 1-2 months of focused effort
- Legendary: 3-6 months or skilled gameplay
- Mythic: 6+ months or extreme dedication

**Reward Philosophy:**
- Never pay-to-win
- Titles are always cosmetic
- Permanent buffs must be small (+5-10% max)
- Exclusive items should be prestigious, not overpowered
- IRF rewards scale with difficulty
